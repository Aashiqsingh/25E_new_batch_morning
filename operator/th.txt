There are three types of operators.
1. unary operators
    - it will required only one operand
    ex : a++
2. binary operators
    - it will required two operands
3. ternary operators
    - it will required three operands


Binary operators:
1. Airthmatic operators : + , - , * , / , %, **
2. Assignment operators : = ,( += , -= , *= , /= , %=)- short hand property
3. relational operator / comparison operator : < , > , <= , >= , == , != , === , !==
4. Logical operators : && , || , !
5. Bitwise operators : & , | , ^
